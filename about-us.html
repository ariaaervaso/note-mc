About Us

History


Nakiya de Freitas is a 22 year old, Music Major from Antigua and Barbuda. She currently attends the Univertisy of the West Indies, St Augustine Campus in Trinidad and Tobago.


Growing up in the village, I always noticed that many children are usually wandering the streets with nothing to do after school. While some may have been involved in sports, I believed that there may be creative outlets for children to express themselves.

When I got involved in the steelpan. I saw its ability to unite persons depite their age or even their background. I first thought over only opening the music school to just children, but I thought about the stories I've heard of adults who also always wanted to learn and instrument.

Nakiya de Freitas
